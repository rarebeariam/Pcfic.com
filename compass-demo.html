How routing works: Every claim is scored by PCF — the structural correlation engine. If confidence is ≥0.70 the claim is served locally at near-zero cost (no LLM call). Below threshold the query routes to Claude. Claude responses cache deterministically, so cost-per-query falls over time as the corpus matures.
