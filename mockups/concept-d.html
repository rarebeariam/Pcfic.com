How It Works

The PCF detection pipeline

Four mathematical steps. No gradient descent. No inference latency budget. Every detection is structurally provable.

01 / Tokenize

Network events to behavioral tokens

Raw packet flows become semantic behavioral tokens — AUTH_BRUTE, NET_FLOOD, NET_PORTSCAN, NET_SLOW_CONN — capturing connection intent, not just raw bytes.

02 / Vectorize

Compute Positional Signature Vector

PCF structural analysis applied over token co-occurrence at offset k. A structural fingerprint — not a learned embedding. (Patent Pending)

03 / Classify

Dual-signal structural comparison

Signal A fires if PSV cosine distance from benign centroid exceeds the 90th percentile. Signal B fires if attack-token fraction exceeds the 99th percentile. OR logic — no tuning required.

04 / Alert

Structured alert with full provenance

Every alert surfaces the PSV vector, cosine distance, token sequence, and signal triggers. Security teams see why a trace was flagged — not just a risk score from a black box.
