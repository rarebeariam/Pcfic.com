How It Works

PCF: Positional Correlation Fields

A patent-pending mathematical framework that encodes network behavior as structural geometry rather than learned features.

01 — TOKENIZE

Network events become behavioral tokens

Raw packet flows are mapped to semantic tokens: AUTH_BRUTE, NET_FLOOD, NET_PORTSCAN, NET_SLOW_CONN, and others — capturing the behavioral fingerprint of each connection.

02 — CORRELATE

Positional Signature Vectors computed

PCF applies proprietary structural scoring over token co-occurrence positions — producing a structural fingerprint for each trace. (Patent Pending)

03 — COMPARE

Structural divergence measured

Attack traces diverge geometrically from the benign PSV centroid. A dual-signal classifier flags anomalies by cosine distance and attack-token density — no learned decision boundary.

04 — ALERT

Sub-2ms detection, zero cloud dependency

Decisions execute in pure Python on commodity hardware in ~1.5ms. Results surface in the Laminar console with structural provenance — not a black-box score.
