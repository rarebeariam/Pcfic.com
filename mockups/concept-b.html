03  /  Technology

How Positional Correlation Fields work

PCF is a mathematical framework — not a neural network. Detection emerges from measurable structural divergence, making results auditable and explainable by design.

Step 01  /  Tokenize

Network events mapped to behavioral tokens

Raw packet flows become semantic tokens: AUTH_BRUTE, NET_FLOOD, NET_PORTSCAN, NET_SLOW_CONN — capturing behavioral fingerprints without raw payload inspection.

Step 02  /  Vectorize

Positional Signature Vectors computed

PCF applies a proprietary structural scoring method over token co-occurrence positions, computing a positional signature for each offset k. (Patent Pending)

Step 03  /  Classify

Structural divergence measured

Attack traces diverge geometrically from the benign PSV centroid. A dual-signal classifier — cosine distance and attack-token density — flags anomalies without a learned decision boundary.

Step 04  /  Alert

Explainable alerts with structural provenance

Every alert includes the structural divergence measure and contributing token signals — not a black-box score. Security engineers see exactly why a trace was flagged.
