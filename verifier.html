How It Works
Step 1
Tokenize
Claim and reference documents are split into word token sequences. Case-normalized, punctuation stripped.
Step 2
PMI Tensor
Co-occurrence statistics computed at offsets k=1..10 with decay kernel K(k)=1/(k+1). Mutual information aggregated per offset.
K(k) = 1/(k+1), k=1..10
Step 3
PSV Comparison
Positional Signature Vector σ_k = mean(PMI_k)/(std(PMI_k)+0.01). Convergence C = 1/(1+σ²) measures structural alignment.
C = 1/(1+σ²)
Step 4
Verdict
SUPPORTED (C > 0.75), REFUTED (contradiction pattern detected), or UNCERTAIN when structural evidence is inconclusive.
