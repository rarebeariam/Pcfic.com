How It Works
Step 1
Confidence Scoring
The query is scored against the current corpus. High score = the answer is already well-covered. Low score = the system isn't confident enough to answer alone.
Step 2
Route Decision
Confidence ≥ 0.70 → CPU path. Served locally in under 2ms at a fraction of GPU cost. Below threshold → routes to GPU for accurate inference.
Step 3
CPU Path
Answer served directly from the corpus. Deterministic, auditable, and cached — repeat queries cost nothing. No LLM call made.
Step 4
Corpus Grows
GPU responses feed back into the corpus automatically. The next similar query routes to CPU. Cost floor drops over time without any manual labeling.
