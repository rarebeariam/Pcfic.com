How It Works
Step 1
RL Agent
Policy network selects candidate documents from a pool. RSSM world model predicts structural quality of each candidate before committing to the corpus.
Step 2
PCF Reward
Convergence C = 1/(1+σ²) computed on the updated corpus. Reward R = clip(log1p(C_new − C_old) / 10, −1, 1). Reward signal is fully structural — no human preference required.
R_PCF = clip(log1p(ΔC)/10, -1, 1)
Step 3
Safety Shield
5-gate veto: (1) convergence improvement, (2) minimum C threshold, (3) topical relevance, (4) no contradictions, (5) non-increasing variance. All 5 must pass.
Step 4
Audit Log
Every accept/reject decision SHA-256 hash-chained with the previous decision. 915 total logged decisions in the measured training run. Fully reproducible.
